Welcome to Danaroo's Capital City Quiz!

Test your knowledge of capital cities of the world! Please enter your name in the text field below:

This website will keep track of your score. Please keep the following in mind when answering the quiz questions:

  • The quiz questions do not exclusively ask about soveriegn states or members of the United Nations.
  • Type using only plain English characters. Use of diacritical marks or accents are not allowed and, while their use may be technically correct, using them will result in a failed quiz question.
  • The quiz is not forgiving when it comes to spelling. Capital names must be spelled exactly right. However, the quiz is NOT case sensitive.
  • In cases of countries that have multiple capitals, the correct answer will be the "official" capital. If there is no official capital, the correct answer will be the "executive" capital.
  • You can 'rate' each quiz question's difficulty as 'easy', 'medium' or 'hard'. Rating the questions is optional, but if you choose to participate you will be contributing to the development of the quiz!

Thanks for playing and enjoy the game!
